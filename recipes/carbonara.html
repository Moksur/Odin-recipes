Creamy Carbonara

What you will need

  • Bacon
  • Pasta
  • 2dl Cooking cream
  • Garlic
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 3dl Cheese for shredding or already shredded

Instructions

  1. Cut the bacon into smaller pieces
  2. Start boiling some water for the pasta
  3. Get a bowl and put about 3dl of cheese and 2dl cooking cream into the bowl
  4. Get a garlic and finely dice it or squeeze it through a garlic press
  5. Mix the garlic, cheese, cooking cream while salting and peppering to your liking
  6. Cook up the bacon, once done put it into the bowl and mix
  7. Once the pasta is done pour the water out and put the pasta in the bowl mix and serve
