Easy Guacamole

What you will need

  • 2 Avocados
  • Garlic
  • 1.5dl Sour cream
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Cut the avocado in half and scoop out the green avocado meat into a tiny bowl
  2. mush it up then put 1.5 dl of sour cream into the bowl and mix
  3. take a garlic and finely slice it or crush it in a garlic press
  4. add salt and mix it all up, test and see if requires more salt or garlic then serve
