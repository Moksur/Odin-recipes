Miso soup

What you will need

  • 1 L water
  • 1 fish stock cube
  • 4 tbsp miso paste
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped leeks
  • 5 thinly sliced large mushrooms
  • 1/2 pack of tofu (each 200 g)

Instructions

  1. Boil the water. Add crumbled fish stock cube. Cook. Add the leek and the mushroom. Simmer for a couple of minutes.
  2. Stir in the miso paste. Taste. If the taste is weak, add more miso. Add diced tofu then serve
